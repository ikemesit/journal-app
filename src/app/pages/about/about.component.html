Aims & Scope

Nigerian Journal of Pharmaceutical and Applied Science Research (NIJOPHASR) is a quarterly publication (March, June, September, and December) and covers all areas of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Chemical and Life Sciences, Medical and Paramedical Sciences.

Editorial Policies

Nigerian Journal of Pharmaceutical and Applied Science Research is published quarterly as reference source to researchers in the Pharmaceutical Science, Chemical and Life Sciences, Medical and Paramedical Sciences. It is a referred journal. Statements and opinions herein expressed in the articles and communication are those of the authors and not the Editor(s) and Publisher. Subscription

Order for current subscription and back issues could be sent to the publishers. Subscriptions are accepted on prepaid basis only. Issues will be sent by surface mail except where a subscriber has an alternative.
